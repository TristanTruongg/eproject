Top 10 most beautiful beaches in the West

Summer is the ideal season to travel, especially sea travel. Imagine, the sun is full, the white sand is smooth and the colorful swimsuits ... What's better than that? webdulichbien.com pick up your backpack and head to one of the 10 "dreamlike" beaches in the western hemisphere under the wires


1. Ipanema Beach, Brazil



This is one of the most popular beaches in Brazil, and if you're a fan of surfing, it's even more impossible to ignore because it's considered the best place to surf.


2. Cabo San Juan Beach, Colombia



Attractive reasons: This is a beautiful nature reserve with a lot of reasons to "hold on" to a nature-loving traveler. Among the beautiful white sands are bright water like crystal light, you will feel extremely relaxed and comfortable by the beauty and peace that Tayrona brings.


3.Akumal Beach, Mexico



Compelling reason: It's one of the caribbean's most luxurious resort towns. Here you will find beautiful beaches, exclusive hotels with unique architecture and delicious food, besides you will also find the perfect balance between nature and entertainment activities at night.


4. Playa Del Amor Beach, Mexico



Compelling reason: This is one of the most visited places when visiting the Mayan Riviera. Here you will also find the famous Quinta Avenida route, Quinta Avenida is the largest tourist route, where you can find anything that can be met such as: From restaurants, clubs, bars, tourist services, to great shopping activities. Make your stay in Playa Del Carmen memorable!


5. West bay Beach, Honduras



Here's why you'll find everything you need to have for a great and relaxing vacation like never before. In addition to the beautiful white sand beaches and majestic scenery of tropical jungles, Roatán also has a diverse and extremely unique coral reef that you have to see firsthand to be able to believe.


6, Santa Teresa Beach, Costa Rica



The reason is fascinating: This is a town located on the Nicoya Peninsula, known as a great place to surf – although this beach is more than a "treasure" hidden because it is not part of Guanacaste Province and is a bit difficult to get around. But once you have decided to take a long trip to Malpaís, you will get the "results" you deserve.


7. Monterrico Beach, Guatemala



The reason is fascinating: Dubbed the best beach in Guatemala, this black sand sea paradise is a place you can't miss when traveling to the land of "eternal spring". Besides its unique volcanic sand color, Monterrico is also extremely bustling with great restaurants and exciting entertainment activities at night.


8. Johnny Cay Beach, Colombia



Reasons for the attraction: The white sandy beach and the smooth, wavy waters of this oasis are a great destination for travelers looking for a feeling of complete relaxation. Moreover, the weather on this island is quite amazing with a chain of high-class hotels and spas where you will be served as a member of the "royal family".


9. Beach Tulum, Mexico



Why it's fascinating: This spectacular beach is a combination of white sands and crystal clear waters – and an archaeological site of the Mayans. Tulum is the ideal place for visitors who want to relax and learn a little more about the ancient Mayan culture.


10. Barra da Lagoa Beach, Brazil



Why it's tempting: If you're looking for a tourist destination that can relax during the day and party at night then you have to go to Barra da Lagoa right away. During the day you can enjoy the beautiful beaches surrounded by greenery and light blue waters, while waiting to watch the sunset fall. Then you can go out at night and enjoy reggae music at any bar in the area.
