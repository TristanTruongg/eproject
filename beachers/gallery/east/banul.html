Coast Ipanema

Many people know this beach not from geography lessons and not from their own travel experience, but thanks to bossa nova's famous jazz song "The Girl From Ipanema" ("Garota de Ipanema").


Its authors, Antonio Carlos Jobim and Vinicius de Moraes, made this coast equally famous as Copacabana. Interestingly, in the translation from Tupi, this compound word means "bad, dangerous waters". In fact, the waves in this area are quite aggressive, so the beach is definitely a place worth visiting for surf lovers, but beware of those who come here just to swim.


Beach description

Banul is one of the best beaches on the protected island of Conor. Solitude allows you to fully enjoy the beauty of the unique Philippine nature. . The picturesque and comfortable coastline complements the vibrant and diverse underwater world. The beach offers unique opportunities not only for relaxing by the water, but also for diving.

The location of the beach explains the lack of many standard tourist amenities here. Of the elements of infrastructure on the shore, you can find only arbors for relaxation. You can arrive on the island of Coron from the neighboring island of Busuang or from Palawan by boat.


When is the best time to go?

The peak season in Philippines lasts from December to May. It is arid and sunny at this time, the temperature rises no higher than +32

°C. The wet season and hot summer lasts from June to October. However, prices during this period are much lower, rain storms occur mainly at night, and during the day you can relax in the shade too.
