Nacpan beach

Nacpan Beach is considered the best not only in El Nido, but throughout the Palawan. This is a very picturesque and deserted, but at the same time well-equipped beach, which seems endless.


Beach description

A wide and extended (4100 m) strip of white sand is washed by bright blue waves of clear and clear sea water. Nacpan beach is located in a large secluded bay of the island, surrounded by green hills. A palm grove stretches parallel to the shore, so on the beach you can sit in natural shade. There are waves on Nacpan, but they do not interfere with swimming. The bottom is sand, without stones and corals. The entry into the water is comfortable and smooth, the shallow water is quite long, so the beach is great for families with kids.

On the beach there is an opportunity to rent sunbeds and umbrellas, but their number is limited. There are cafes and shops selling food and drinks. The kitchen closes at sunset, but the bar is open much longer. There is parking for which you will have to pay a small amount. There is a volleyball court right on the beach, and a little further away is a paintball area. Near the coast is one of the beautiful waterfalls of Palawan - Nagkalit-kalit.


When is the best time to go?

The peak season in Philippines lasts from December to May. It is arid and sunny at this time, the temperature rises no higher than +32

°C. The wet season and hot summer lasts from June to October. However, prices during this period are much lower, rain storms occur mainly at night, and during the day you can relax in the shade too.
