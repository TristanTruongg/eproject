Tulum Beach

It is located in a distance from the city of Tulum, about six kilometers. The beach is covered with fine sand, with minor waves in calm and sunny weather. Strong winds reach the coastline at the end of the season - in April, and rains before the start of the high period - in September.


Beach description

More suitable for measured rest - yoga classes, walks along the beach. A rare tourist can rent of kayak or increase the level of adrenaline on kitesurfing. There are not many places of entertainment in the vast territory, mostly the whole coastline is occupied by ecological hotels or bungalows with an average level of service. If choosing a budget accommodation in the city, then it is possible to get to the beach by taxi or rented bike. And tourists arrive in the city from the airport in Cancun by bus.

It is possible to diversify daily look at the ideal turquoise surface of the Caribbean by visiting the city itself for shopping or viewing local sights - a temple with an exposition of ancient sculptures and frescoes, archaeological ruins or a settlement of the Pueblo Indians.


When is the best time to go?

From May to October, heavy rains and tornadoes are usually played off the Mexican coasts. So the best time to visit the country is from November to March. However, it should be mindful of that prices in the high season are higher than usual.
