Playa del Amor beach

Hidden Beach, Playa del Amor, Love Beach - these are all the names of one famous beach in Mexico with the romantic name Love Beach, which is located in a hinged cave. As the Mexicans themselves admit, this place is not at all a miracle of nature, but the result of a test of military weapons and equipment.


Beach description

The beach got its name for a reason, this place is a favorite vacation spot for couples in love and newlyweds. Getting to the mysterious beach of love is a real adventure. To get to the beach it is necessary to go by boat from Puerto Vallarta to the north-west to the mouth of Banderas Bay for about an hour, located at the base of Marieta Island. It is easy to understand, that Love Beach is nearby by the entry to the sandy cave, swim another 150 m through the cave archipelago and here it is, Love Beach.

Amazing snow-white sands and quiet turquoise of ocean breezes are located in a deep cave, that hangs over the beach. The cave is more like a huge crater in the cliff, full of mystery and inscrutability. The cave is also a kind of parasol, that shades part of the beach area.


In 2005, Love Beach received the title of National Park Parque Nacional Islas Marietas. Such a decision was a necessity, because in some periods, about 3,000 tourists could visit a small beach per day. This became a danger to the destruction of this beautiful place, so the authorities decided to restrict visits to the Love Beach, giving it the title and restricting his visit. No more than 120 people a day are allowed to enter the beach. In 2008, the beach was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
