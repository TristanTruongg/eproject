Coast Ipanema

Many people know this beach not from geography lessons and not from their own travel experience, but thanks to bossa nova's famous jazz song "The Girl From Ipanema" ("Garota de Ipanema").


Its authors, Antonio Carlos Jobim and Vinicius de Moraes, made this coast equally famous as Copacabana. Interestingly, in the translation from Tupi, this compound word means "bad, dangerous waters". In fact, the waves in this area are quite aggressive, so the beach is definitely a place worth visiting for surf lovers, but beware of those who come here just to swim.


Description of the sea

The beach stretches 2 km between Hotel Jardim de Alah and Arpoador Beach, which is separated from Copacabana by a rocky tip of the same name. It is considered a place of entertainment by the elite, partly because of the price, partly because of a kind of local "club", made up of artists and musicians.

The beach territory is a flat, conditional sandy shore divided into several sections, marked by the number:


8 - children's area, a place that is loved by couples, enjoys quiet and unrestricted leisure time;


9 - a gathering place of hippies and other travel enthusiasts with concerts and dances until dawn, as well as an entertainment area for gender minorities;


10 - a plot where lovers of outdoor activities can go wild, since here you can play football, volleyball and play frescoes. The water and sand here are cleaner than in Copacabana. This is explained by more intense waves, better organization and order on the beach. In fact, the waves here are very strong, sometimes even rushing to the shore. They strongly "pull" you back, so be careful and only swim in places where locals do it. Don't let the kids into the water alone.


The depth begins gradually, the bottom is without any cliffs. But due to the high and frequent waves, it is difficult to recognize the depth and it is constantly changing.


When is the best time to go?

The best time to visit Brazil - is in spring and autumn. During this period, mild and dry weather reigned here. In winter, there is heat and heavy rain. And in the summer (from June to August), the lowest temperatures are recorded in the country.

Infrastructure

Ipanema Beach is a lively and well-equipped beach that is not only famous and fashionable for relaxation but also comfort. Sun loungers, toilets, umbrellas, showers are things that you should not take for granted as the advantage of the beach. But what really sets it apart from other areas of the beach are the high-end boutiques, nightclubs and trendy restaurants that sit on the sand, if not on the sand, then maybe. walk from the beach entrance.

One of the most famous and charming hotels is considered the Arena Ipanema Hotel 4*. In cafes you will be offered a wide selection of dishes. Feijoada is definitely worth a try - spicy stewed pork with beans, which is usually served with a side dish, which includes chopped oranges, fried eggs, cassava flour, thinly sliced ​​cabbage and white rice. In kiosks located on the beach you will find coconut water, corn on the cob, fried shrimp, baked cheese rolls and other snacks. It is traditional to drink all this with a cocktail "caipirinha", made with Brazilian rum from sugar cane, lemon and sugar.

Khi chọn các hoạt động trên bãi biển, du khách có thể chọn các hoạt động trên cạn và dưới nước. Vĩ độ của bãi biển cho phép bạn chơi các môn thể thao đồng đội, chẳng hạn như bóng đá, bóng chuyền hoặc bóng chày. Frescobole (đó là quần vợt bãi biển) đặc biệt phổ biến, nó được chơi bằng vợt gỗ và bóng cao su. Và, tất nhiên, làm thế nào để ở trên bãi biển Rio và không nhìn thấy những người lướt sóng? Lướt sóng và lướt ván diều ở đây sẽ gây ấn tượng ngay cả với một người có kinh nghiệm chinh phục sóng, bởi vì sóng ở đây lớn đến mức bạn chỉ cần có thời gian là có thể bắt được chúng.
