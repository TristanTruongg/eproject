Top 10 most beautiful beaches in the East

This summer you do not need to go too far to maldives or Hawaii, in Southeast Asia, these countries very close to Vietnam also have beautiful paradise islands like dreams dubbed "raw gems" due to retaining the pristine beauty of people. Now, let's explore the Top 10 beautiful Southeast Asian beaches below to schedule a very exciting summer.


1. Banul Beach, Philippines



Banul Beach is a beautiful place in the Philippines. White sand here is washed by crystal clear aquamarine waters, from which dark sheer cliffs “grow”


2. Las Cabanas, Philippines



El Nido recently reached the top 4 on Conde Nast Travelers' list of "20 most beautiful beaches in the world". If you are looking to explore nature, see the limestone cliffs, enjoy the turquoise water or sunbathe on the white sandy beaches, El Nido is the best choice for you.

The island also has a multitude of activities at sea such as kayaking or diving under cliffs. Choose your favorite activities and experience the best in El Nido!


3.Nai Harn Beach, Phuket, Thailand



Nai Harn Beach is one of Phuket's top beaches located in the south of Phuket Island. The unspoiled look of the beach is sure to please you if you are looking for a fresh and quiet place for this summer.

You can also take part in scuba diving as well as windsurfing on the east and west sides of the beach. Most days of the week, at Nai Harn Beach is quite quiet and suitable for visitors to only swim gently or lie in the sun.


4. Beach Phra Nang, Railay, Thailand



Phra Nang Beach is located at the southern tip of Railay, a peninsula located in the Andaman Sea. Phra Nang Beach is famous for its white sand stretches along with cliffs blocking the two ends of the beach. Especially here, food and drinks are sold right on the boats along the beach. You can choose a beer, lie in the sun and watch people try their hand at mountaineering.


5. Lombok, Indonesia



If you're looking for a vacation in the tropics like your dream, get ready for a trip to explore Lombok, a world far from the noisy world of other famous beaches. Spend the day immersed in the turquoise blue water, the white sands at your feet and enjoy the freshly baked seafood near the local restaurant.


6, Beach Otres, Sihanoukville, Campuchia



Otres Beach is known for many small fishing villages located close to the sea, smooth white sand in the shape of a semicircular length up to 3km long and clear blue water. Otres' villages and temples are also only about 1km from the beach so if you want to learn more about the lives of the indigenous people, you can go there by route from Ochheuteal across the hill is to arrive. The life of the people here is mainly based on fishing and trading tourism services although small but the quality of service is quite good. In addition, the nightlife at this beach is extremely bustling with trendy restaurant bars. This is the perfect choice if you want to have a busy summer.


7. Koh Rong Samloem, Campuchia



Koh Rong Samloem is a separate island about 2 hours by train from the sihanoukville mainland. Coming to Koh Rong Saloem, you will feel everything in the chaos of the city seems to be forgotten. This island is still very unspoiled, without much human impact, so the natural beauty remains intact. Setting foot on the island, you will surely be surprised by the clear water, the white sand is smooth and extremely clean.


8. Teluk Nipah, Pangkor, Malaysia



Pangkor Island is a small island located on the west coast of Malaysia. One of pangkor island's beautiful beaches is Teluk Nipah Beach which attracts a lot of visitors because of its unspoiled and peaceful beauty. Here you can participate in a lot of water sports games or boat tours, diving to watch fish. In particular, right next to the beach is Lin Ke Kong Temple, a small but beautifully located temple because it is located right next to the arc-shaped coast, and you can also see the small Prison Island opposite from here as well.


9. Tanjung Rhu, Langkawi, Malaysia



Tanjung Rhu impresses with its long, white and extremely clean beach. Unlike the beauty of the Maldives-style beaches, the sea in front of Tanjung Rhu is not endlessly empty but adorned with islands and mountain ranges. The sea water here is not turquoise, as colorful as the Maldives but the waves are very small, a great place for anyone who wants to soak in the cool blue water to relax. If you come here when the tide recedes, you can admire a long sand whip running straight to the remote islands.


10. Ngapali beach, Ngapali, Myanmar



This beach is famous for its natural beauty, pure white sands and tall coconut trees and beautiful turquoise waters. This is a beach that has just been known in Myanmar so it is deserted, very suitable for you to rest and relax in peace. However, there are still activities for you to warm up your summer such as walking on the beach, cycling, boating, golfing, scuba diving and surfing.
